# HOST-EXAM Trial: 10-Year Follow-Up

**Published:** March 29, 2026 | *The Lancet*  
**Authors:** Koo BK, Kang J, Park KW, et al.  
**Trial Registration:** NCT02044250

---

## Study Overview (PICO Format)

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **P - Population** | Adults who completed 12±6 months DAPT after DES-PCI without clinical events (East Asian, mean age 63.5, 25% female) |
| **I - Intervention** | Clopidogrel monotherapy 75 mg/day (n=2,710) |
| **C - Comparison** | Aspirin monotherapy 100 mg/day (n=2,728) |
| **O - Outcome** | Composite: all-cause death, non-fatal MI, stroke, ACS readmission, major bleeding |

---

## Key Results: 10-Year Follow-Up

| Outcome | Clopidogrel | Aspirin | ARR | p-value |
|---------|-------------|---------|-----|---------|
| **Primary Composite** | 25.4% | 28.5% | 3.1% | <0.01 |
| Stroke | 4.6% | 6.4% | 1.8% | <0.01 |
| Major Bleeding | 5.6% | 7.7% | 2.1% | <0.01 |
| ACS Readmission | 8.7% | 11.0% | 2.3% | <0.01 |

## Clinical Implications

- ✅ **Biphasic benefit:** Reduces both thrombotic AND bleeding risks simultaneously
- ✅ Longest randomized comparison of antiplatelet monotherapy to date
- ✅ Supports clopidogrel as alternative to aspirin for long-term secondary prevention post-PCI

---

## Limitations

- ⚠️ Study population: East Asian patients only
- ⚠️ Clopidogrel resistance not assessed
- ⚠️ Generalizability to other populations unclear
