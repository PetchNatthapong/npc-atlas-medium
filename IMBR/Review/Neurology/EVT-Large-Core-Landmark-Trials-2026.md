# EVT in Large Core Infarction: PICO-Based Comprehensive Review
## Expert Review | April 2026

**Author:** NPC  
**Sources:** NEJM, Lancet Neurology, JAMA, Stroke

---

## Abstract

**Background:** EVT in large core infarction was previously excluded. Six RCTs published 2022-2024 have established EVT as a reasonable treatment option.

**Key Findings:** 5 of 6 trials demonstrated significant benefit. **NNT = 6-13** for functional independence. No significant increase in symptomatic ICH.

### 1. RESCUE-JAPAN LIMIT (2022)

**Study Design:** Multicenter, Open-label, RCT, PROBE | **n=203**

**Citation:** Yoshimura S, et al. *N Engl J Med.* 2022;386:1303-1313. doi:10.1056/NEJMoa2118191

#### P (Population)
- **n = 203** (101 EVT, 102 Control)
- Acute ischemic stroke with **LVO (ICA or M1)**
- **ASPECTS 3-5** on NCCT or DWI-MRI
- **NIHSS ≥6**
- **Pre-stroke mRS 0-1**
- Age ≥18 years
- Onset ≤6h (or ≤24h if FLAIR negative)
- Randomization within 60 min of imaging

**Exclusion:**
- Significant mass effect with midline shift
- Acute intracranial hemorrhage
- High hemorrhage risk (platelets <40,000, APTT >50 sec, PT-INR >3.0)

#### I (Intervention)
- **EVT:** Stent retriever, aspiration catheter, balloon angioplasty, or intracranial stent
- **IV alteplase:** 0.6 mg/kg (Japanese dose)

#### C (Control)
- Medical management alone (± IV alteplase if indicated)

#### O (Outcome)

**Primary:** mRS 0-3 at 90 days

| Outcome | EVT (n=101) | Control (n=102) | OR (95% CI) | P-value | NNT |
|---------|--------------|-----------------|-------------|---------|-----|
| **mRS 0-3 (Primary)** | **36%** | **26%** | **—** | **P=0.02** | **10** |
| mRS 0-2 at 90d | 9.5% | 0% | — | — | — |
| **mRS shift (Secondary)** | — | — | **OR 1.42 (0.88-2.29)** | **P=0.15 (NS)** | — |
| Symptomatic ICH | 9% | 5% | — | P=0.24 | — |

> ✅ Primary outcome significant, secondary (mRS shift) not significant

**Citation:** Li Q, et al. *N Engl J Med.* 2023;388:1240-1251. doi:10.1056/NEJMoa2213379

#### P (Population)
- **n = 456** (46 centers, China)
- **LVO (ICA or M1)**
- **ASPECTS 3-5** or **core 70-100 mL** on CTP or DWI-MRI

**Exclusion:**
- Pre-stroke mRS >2
- Intracranial hemorrhage
- Mass effect with midline shift >5 mm

#### I (Intervention)

#### C (Control)
- Medical management alone

### 6. LASTE (2024)

**Study Design:** Multicenter, Randomized, Open-label, Adjudicator-blinded | **n=324** (159 EVT, 165 Control)

**Citation:** Moret C, et al. *N Engl J Med.* 2024;390:1677-1689. doi:10.1056/NEJMoa2314063

#### P (Population)
- **n = 324** (159 EVT, 165 Control)
- **LVO (ICA or M1)**
- **ASPECTS ≤5** on NCCT or DWI (>3 if age >80)
- **NIHSS ≥6** (median: 21)
- Pre-stroke mRS ≤1
- **Onset ≤6.5 hours** (or ≤24h if FLAIR negative)
- Median ASPECTS: 2

**Exclusion:**
- Intracerebral hemorrhage or midline shift
- Cervical ICA disease requiring stent
- Creatinine >4.0 mg/dL

#### I (Intervention)
- **EVT + Medical therapy**
- Median time from onset to thrombectomy: 305 minutes

#### C (Control)
- Medical therapy alone

#### O (Outcome)

**Primary:** Median mRS score at 90 days

| Outcome | EVT (n=159) | Control (n=165) | OR (95% CI) | P-value | NNT |
|---------|--------------|-----------------|-------------|---------|-----|
| **mRS median (Primary)** | **4** | **6** | **1.63 (1.29-2.06)** | **P<0.001** | **~7** |
| **All-cause death 90d** | **36.1%** | **55.5%** | **RR 0.65 (0.50-0.84)** | **P<0.001** | **5** |
| Symptomatic ICH | 9.6% | 5.7% | RR 1.73 (0.78-4.68) | NS | — |

> ✅ First trial to show significant mortality reduction (NNT=5 for survival)

## 💡 Take-home Points

1. **5 of 6 trials showed significant benefit**
2. **TESLA was the only negative trial** — NCCT-only may be insufficient
3. **NNT = 6-13** for functional independence
4. **No significant increase in symptomatic ICH** (P=0.10)
5. **LASTE showed mortality reduction** — RR 0.65, NNT=5
